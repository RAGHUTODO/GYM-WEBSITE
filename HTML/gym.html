Intermediate Gym Plan: Push Pull Legs Split

Your 3-day split for muscle building and strength

Push Day

Muscle groups: Chest, Shoulders, Triceps

  • Bench Press – 3 sets of 8-12 reps
  • Overhead Shoulder Press – 3 sets of 8-12 reps
  • Incline Dumbbell Press – 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Tricep Dips – 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Lateral Raises – 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Pull Day

Muscle groups: Back, Biceps, Rear Deltoids

  • Pull-Ups – 4 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Barbell Rows – 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Seated Cable Rows – 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Face Pulls – 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Bicep Curls – 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Leg Day

Muscle groups: Quadriceps, Hamstrings, Glutes, Calves

  • Squats – 4 sets of 8-12 reps
  • Romanian Deadlifts – 3 sets of 10-12 reps
  • Leg Press – 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Calf Raises – 3 sets of 15-20 reps
  • Hamstring Curls – 3 sets of 10-15 reps

Tips & Recovery

  • Warm up before every session
  • Stretch after every workout
  • Rest at least one day after finishing each full cycle
  • Eat balanced meals with sufficient protein
