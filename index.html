Intermediate Gym Plan

Push Pull Legs Split Workout Routine

Push Day

Target muscles: Chest, Shoulders, Triceps

  • Barbell Bench Press – 3 sets of 6-10 reps
  • Incline Dumbbell Press – 3 sets of 8-12 reps
  • Seated Overhead Press – 3 sets of 8-10 reps
  • Lateral Raises – 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Tricep Rope Pushdown – 3 sets of 12-15 reps

Pull Day

Target muscles: Back, Biceps, Rear Deltoids

  • Deadlift – 3 sets of 5-8 reps
  • Pull-Ups – 3 sets of 6-10 reps
  • Seated Cable Rows – 3 sets of 8-12 reps
  • Face Pulls – 3 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Barbell Bicep Curls – 3 sets of 10-12 reps

Leg Day

Target muscles: Quadriceps, Hamstrings, Glutes, Calves

  • Back Squats – 3 sets of 6-10 reps
  • Romanian Deadlifts – 3 sets of 8-12 reps
  • Leg Press – 3 sets of 10-15 reps
  • Calf Raises – 4 sets of 12-15 reps
  • Hamstring Curls – 3 sets of 10-15 reps
